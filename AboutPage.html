About MealMatch

MealMatch is a recipe finder app that allows users to search for recipes based on an extensive list of filters. Users can view recipes that meet their desired qualities, such as calorie count, type of meal, region of origin, and more. Users can also save favorite recipes and purchase ingredients directly through the app for convenience.

Mission Statement

At MealMatch, we believe that everyone should have access to delicious and healthy meals. Our mission is to provide a platform that makes it easy for people to find and prepare nutritious meals that fit their dietary needs and preferences. We strive to make healthy eating enjoyable and accessible to all.
